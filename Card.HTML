Leopard Gecko

Leopard Geckos are tiny lizards who look like they are always smiling!

  • Origins: Afghanistan and North India
  • Size: 7 to 10 inches
  • Diet: Live insects!
  • Average Lifespan: 6 to 10 years

A pet gecko can range in cost from $20 to $3,000! The longest living pet gecko is 27.5 years old. Geckos do not eat plants. They the best pet to have if you have children, as they are easy to care for and very gentle and friendly.
